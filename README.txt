Interaction Instructions:
The goal is to hit the other player's ship with a rocket before they hit you.
You can only have one rocket on the screen at a time, so wait for your first one to leave
before trying to fire again.

Player 1 controls:
W - Move forward
A - Turn left
D - Turn right
Space - Fire

Player 2 controls:
UP - Move forward
LEFT - Turn left
RIGHT - Turn right
Left Click - Fire
